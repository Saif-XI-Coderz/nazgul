About Us

Android Land is a huge futuristic amusement park. We exist since 2017. The park is populated with real-life androids. Along with the decor, they recreate various immersive universes based on different themes.


Have a look at the inside of our androids! They are built using cutting-edge technology and artificial intelligence.


The realism is such that you won't even notice the difference. They perfectly portray their characters and were cautiously programmed and are constantly updated to ensure that you receive the best experience at Android Land in all guaranteed fun and safety.

Our current themes include Monster's Mayhem, Heroes City and History Warriors. You will be able walk among real monsters without being eaten or killed, get to be around your favourite superheroes and see warriors fighting just as they fought throughout history.

More themes will soon be available, based on public demand.
